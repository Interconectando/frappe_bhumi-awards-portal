How?

Nominations

Awards team invites applications. Colleges can self-nominate themselves or the public/students can nominate/vote for specific colleges.

Applications

Nominated colleges are reached out to and asked to submit details in a detailed form about their effort and initiatives to be socially conscious.

Selection

A 3-5 membered-jury consisting would be evaluating the nominations based on fixed criteria and details submitted by the college. Final awardees are selected based on the college’s score in specific criteria and the number of public votes.

Award Ceremony

All the nominated colleges and the awardees are invited for the final virtual award ceremony where awards are given and guest speeches are delivered on making Indian colleges more socially responsible.

Criteria

The following are the criteria with which the jury members will be evaluating the nominations from colleges. Specifics about each criteria bucket and details would be decided by the expert panel consisting of subject matter experts & NGOs.

  • Student groups around social responsibility
  • Volunteerism in college
  • Students as social change leaders
  • Energy Consumption & Sources
  • Greenery in Campus
  • Effective usage of Water
  • Waste management - segregation and disposal (non-food)
  • Food waste - Tracking and reduction
  • No alcohol and substance campus
  • Diversity & Inclusion
  • Policies around social responsibility
  • Policies around Environment
  • Number of staff working on social responsibility (partially or completely)
